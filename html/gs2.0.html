Courses

Baby Khalsa Club

  • Teaching through singing
  • Arts and crafts activities
  • Singing Gurbani through Kirtan

Little Khalsa Club

  • Levels 1-2
  • Interactive activites
  • Learning Penti
  • Building a lasting relationship with Maharaji Ji

Gurmukhi

  • Levels 1-4
  • Curriculum based
  • Gain confidence in reading, writing and speaking

Electives

Santhya

  • Levels 1-3: Nitnem, Panj Granthi, SGGSJ
  • Study and practice Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji
  • Understand the language and history

Gatka

  • Teaching of traditional Sikh martial arts
  • Development of students' physical fitness, mental clarity, discipline and respect of the art.
  • Encouraged to practice and participate in live demonstrations

Gurbani Kirtan

  • Connecting youth with Gurbani at a deeper level
  • Curriculum based on Raags
  • Understand the meaning behind Kirtan and Raags
